The ongoing AE: Artificial Emotions project sets out to develop a robot capable of creating drawings based on human emotions. The project envisages the harnessing of human self-expression and the visual language communication capabilities of robots. The primary purpose of this effort is to explore the potential benefits of a human/robot creative collaboration on mental health and well being.



Background: The Benefits of Human Self-Expression through Drawing.

Drawing is a fundamental form of human expression with deep-rooted psychological benefits. It serves as a powerful tool for individuals to externalise and communicate their thoughts and emotions, fostering self-awareness and emotional well-being. Artistic expression, including drawing, has been linked to stress reduction, improved mood, and enhanced cognitive abilities. In our rapidly advancing technological landscape, society is increasingly recognising the importance of emotional well-being. The prevalence of stress, anxiety, and mental health issues highlights the need for innovative approaches that leverage technology to enhance emotional expression and self-awareness.
